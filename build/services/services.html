Our Services

Representation in Criminal Court

We provide comprehensive legal representation in criminal matters, ensuring expert defense and guidance through complex legal proceedings.

Family Law

We appreciate and understand that when clients approach us for advice following the breakdown of a marriage or relationship, it is a particularly upsetting and difficult time. Our experienced team will do their best to guide you through the process whether it is preparing an agreement dealing with financial matters or making arrangements for your children. We can also assist in the preparation of “pre-nup” agreements to avoid difficulties later.

Child Law

Our team regularly appear in the civil courts and children’s hearings representing all parties involved whether they are children, parents, grandparents etc. Billy Warden is a Safeguarder to children within the Children’s Hearing system and he, Linda Clark and Cheryl McKnight are all regularly called upon by the courts to prepare reports in cases where residence and contact with children are in dispute or in Permanence Order/Adoption proceedings cases.

Residential Property

We can assist with all matters relating to the sale or purchase of residential property to include the preparation or discharge of mortgage documentation.

Wills, Power of Attorney & Executry

We can assist with planning for the future in the preparation of wills and Power of Attorney documents. We will also respectfully advise regarding the winding up of a deceased’s estate at a time which can be difficult for all.
